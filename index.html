Our mission is to advance the art and science of landscape architecture in the Philippines, promoting sustainable and resilient environments that enhance the well-being of communities. We are dedicated to fostering collaboration, innovation, and professional excellence among our members, while advocating for the responsible stewardship of our natural and built landscapes."

PALA envisions a future where the landscapes of the Philippines are harmoniously designed, resilient to change, and contribute to the overall quality of life. We aspire to be a leading force in shaping environments that inspire, connect, and sustain communities. Through our commitment to excellence, education, and advocacy, PALA aims to be a driving force in the transformation of landscapes for the benefit of present and future generations.
