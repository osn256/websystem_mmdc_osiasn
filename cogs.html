Certificate of good standing

COGS Process

  1. Click the Dues Inquiry icon to check your balance.
  2. Click CPD Inquiry to check your available and required PALA CPD points (Required total CPD points: 15 COD Points).
  3. Deposit the required amount due only to Philippine Association of Landscape Architects (PALA) bank accounts:

    Account Name: Philippine Association of Landscape Architects

    Account Number: BDO Current Account - 1208-0005-31

  4. Submit payment after the receipt of available balance.
  5. Fill up the COGS application form.
  6. Wait for the release of your certificate after 5 working days from the date of application.
  7. An email will be sent to you to notify the availability of your COGS.
